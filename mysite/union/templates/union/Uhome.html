Who are Student's Union

Alexandria Engineering Students Union is a group of students whose aim is to help students in the college
and provide services to them and is a representative of them
The student union consists of 70 students and 7 committees.
Each committee has 10 members, including the secretary and assistant secretary of the committee and
the council of the union Consisting of the President of the Union and his deputy,
and the secretaries and secretaries of assistants
